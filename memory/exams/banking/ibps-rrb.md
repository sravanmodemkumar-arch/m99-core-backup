exam_id: IBPS-RRB-CLERK-MAIN-{YEAR}

## Key Notes
- For Regional Rural Banks across India
- Local language (state language) proficiency required
- RRB PO: Interview after Mains | RRB Clerk: No interview
- Graduation required
