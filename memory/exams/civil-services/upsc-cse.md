## Stage 3 — Personality Test (Interview)
275 marks | Combined with Mains for final merit

## Subject Codes
GK, CURR, ENV, HIST, GEO, ECO, POL, REAS, ENG

## QID Types
S (mcq_single) for Prelims | D (descriptive) for Mains

## Key Notes
- 6 attempts for General (32 years max age)
- Optional subjects: 25+ available (History, Geography, Pub Ad, Sociology, Law, MATH, etc.)
- Prelims: qualifying; Mains + Interview = final rank
