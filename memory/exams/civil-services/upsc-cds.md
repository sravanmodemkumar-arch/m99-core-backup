# UPSC CDS (Combined Defence Services)

board_id  : UPSC
category  : C
exam_id   : UPSC-CDS-{YEAR}-{1|2}-{IMA|INA|AFA|OTA}
pattern_id: CDS-IMA-PAT-V2 / CDS-OTA-PAT-V2

## Sessions
- CDS 1 → Feb exam | CDS 2 → Sep exam

## Pattern — IMA / INA / AFA (3 papers)
duration: 120 min per paper | neg: -1/3

| Paper | Subject | Q | Marks |
|---|---|---|---|
| Paper 1 | English | 120 | 1 |
| Paper 2 | General Knowledge | 120 | 1 |
| Paper 3 | Elementary Mathematics | 100 | 1 |

## Pattern — OTA (2 papers only, no Maths)
| Paper | Subject | Q | Marks |
|---|---|---|---|
| Paper 1 | English | 120 | 1 |
| Paper 2 | General Knowledge | 120 | 1 |

## Subject Codes
ENG, GK, MATH

## QID Types
S (mcq_single)

## Key Notes
- Eligibility: Graduation (IMA/INA/AFA), Any Degree (OTA)
- SSB interview after written
- OTA: for Short Service Commission (men + women)
