## Key Notes
- For Flying Branch, Technical Branch, Ground Duty Branch
- EKT (Engineering Knowledge Test) extra for Technical branch: 45 min, 50Q
- AFSB interview after written
- Age: 20–26 years (Flying), 20–26 (Technical), 20–26 (Ground Duty)
