## Subject Codes
ENG, PHY, CHEM, MATH, BIO, CS, ACCT, ECO, HIST, GEO

## QID Types
SA (short answer) | LA (long answer) | D (descriptive/essay)

## Key Notes
- No negative marking; no MCQ-heavy pattern (more analytical/descriptive)
- Pass: 35% in each subject + English compulsory pass
- ISC is considered more rigorous than CBSE Class 12 in depth
- Well-recognized for college admissions; some colleges give preference
- Practical exams conducted internally + externally moderated by CISCE
- Best 4 subjects (excluding English) considered for merit calculations at many colleges
