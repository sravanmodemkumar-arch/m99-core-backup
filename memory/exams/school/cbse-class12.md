## Key Notes
- No negative marking
- Pass: 33% in each subject
- Board results used for JEE, NEET, CUET eligibility and college admissions
- 5 subjects mandatory; 6th subject can improve aggregate
- CUET (Common University Entrance Test) now required for central university admissions
- Marks moderation applied for maintaining historical pass rates
