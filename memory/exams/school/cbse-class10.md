# CBSE Class 10 Board Exam

board_id  : CBSE
category  : C
exam_id   : CBSE-10-{YEAR}
pattern_id: CBSE-10-PAT-V3

## Exam Pattern
exam_id: CBSE-10-{YEAR}
neg: 0 | Theory: 80 marks + Internal: 20 marks per subject

| Subject | Theory | Internal | Total |
|---|---|---|---|
| English (Language & Literature) | 80 | 20 | 100 |
| Hindi A / Hindi B | 80 | 20 | 100 |
| Mathematics Standard / Basic | 80 | 20 | 100 |
| Science | 80 | 20 | 100 |
| Social Science | 80 | 20 | 100 |
| Additional Subject (IT/Sanskrit/etc.) | 80 | 20 | 100 |

## Question Types per Subject
Science: MCQ(16) + Assertion-Reason(4) + Short Answer(24) + Long Answer(36)
Math: MCQ(20) + Assertion-Reason(4) + Short Answer(24) + Long Answer(32)

## Subject Codes
ENG, HINDI, MATH, SCI, SST

## QID Types
S (mcq_single) | AR (assertion-reason) | SA (short answer) | LA (long answer)

## Key Notes
- No negative marking
- Board determines pass: 33% in each subject theory + internal combined
- 2 variants of Math: Standard (for Class 11 Maths stream) and Basic
- Practical exams: Science(20 marks internal lab), Social(20 marks internal project)
- CBSE term-wise pattern may vary year to year
- Used for admission to Class 11 and competitive exams eligibility
