## QID Types
S (mcq_single) | SA (short answer) | LA (long answer)

## Key Notes
- Conducted by CISCE (Council for Indian School Certificate Examinations)
- More detailed syllabus than CBSE — essay-based answers
- 6 subjects minimum; best 5 considered for aggregate
- No negative marking
- Pass: 35% in aggregate + 35% in each compulsory subject
- Recognized for admissions to all Indian colleges; ISC for Class 12
