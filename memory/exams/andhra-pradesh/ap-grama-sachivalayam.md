## Key Notes
- No negative marking
- Unique to AP — village-level service delivery initiative by AP government
- One of the largest recruitment drives in AP history (15,000+ posts)
- Intermediate (Class 12) or ITI required (varies by post)
- Age: 18–42 years; local residence preference
- Computer-based test; Telugu medium available
- Certificate verification + physical fitness check after selection
