## Key Notes
- Conducted by SLPRB (State Level Police Recruitment Board) AP
- Posts: Civil SI, AR SI, APSP SI, Finger Print SI, Mahila SI
- Graduation required
- Age: 18–26 years (relaxation for reserved categories)
- PET qualifying; final merit based on written test only
- Medical examination after shortlisting
