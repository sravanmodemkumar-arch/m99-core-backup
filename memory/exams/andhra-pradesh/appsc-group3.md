## Key Notes
- Entry-level administrative posts in AP government departments
- No interview — merit based on written test
- Intermediate (Class 12) required for most posts
- Age: 18–42 years (relaxation for reserved categories)
- AP-specific GK questions on welfare schemes and state governance
- Typing test conducted separately for relevant posts
