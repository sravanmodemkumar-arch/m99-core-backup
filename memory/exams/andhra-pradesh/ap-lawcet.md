## QID Types

## Key Notes
- No negative marking
- Gateway for LLB admissions in all AP law colleges
- LAWCET: Graduation required | PGLCET: LLB required
- Conducted by APSCHE
- Rank-based counseling for seat allotment in AP
- Aptitude for Law section tests logical/legal reasoning — no prior law knowledge for LAWCET
- Same structure as TS LAWCET — separate rank lists for AP admissions
