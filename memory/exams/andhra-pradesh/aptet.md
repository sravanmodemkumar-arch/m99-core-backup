## QID Types

## Key Notes
- No negative marking
- Qualifying marks: 60% (BC: 55%, SC/ST/PH: 50%)
- Certificate valid: lifetime (revised from 7 years)
- Mandatory for AP government and government-aided school teaching
- Conducted by Board of Secondary Education, AP
- Both Paper 1 and Paper 2 can be attempted together
