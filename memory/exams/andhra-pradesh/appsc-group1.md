## Key Notes
- AP Public Service Commission — premier state civil services exam
- Posts: Deputy Collector, DSP, Commercial Tax Officer, etc.
- Strong AP-specific GS (Amaravati, AP reorganization, AP economy)
- Graduation required; Age: 18–42 years (relaxation for reserved categories)
- Mains shortlisting: 1:3 ratio per post
