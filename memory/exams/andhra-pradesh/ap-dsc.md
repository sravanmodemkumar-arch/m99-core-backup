GK(10) + Education(10) + Language Content+Method(80) = 100 marks

## PET (Physical Education Teacher)
GK(10) + Education(10) + Physical Education(80) = 100 marks

## Subject Codes
GS, CDP, SUBJ, LANG, PHY-ED

## QID Types
S (mcq_single)

## Key Notes
- APTET qualifying mandatory before DSC
- No interview — merit: written test (80%) + academic marks (20%)
- D.Ed. required for SGT | B.Ed. required for SA/LP
- Conducted by AP government based on vacancies
- AP-specific pedagogy and state education policy content in CDP section
