exam_id: APPSC-AEE-{DEPT}-{YEAR}

| Section | Subject | Q | Marks |
|---|---|---|---|
| General Studies & Mental Ability | GS | 50 | 50 |
| Technical Subject (Engineering) | TECH | 100 | 100 |

Departments: Civil, Electrical, Mechanical, Electronics & Communication, Agriculture

## Subject Codes
GS, TECH (CIVIL / ELEC / MECH / ECE / AGRI)

## QID Types
S (mcq_single)

## Key Notes
- Posts in: R&B, Irrigation, AP TRANSCO, APSPDCL/APEPDCL, APGENCO, PR&RD
- B.Tech / B.E. in respective branch required
- Age: 18–42 years
- Separate notifications per department with different technical syllabi
- No interview for AEE posts (some depts may have interview for senior posts)
- AP-specific infrastructure projects may appear in GS section
