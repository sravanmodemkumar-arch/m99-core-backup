# AP ICET (Integrated Common Entrance Test — MBA/MCA)

board_id  : APSCHE
category  : C
exam_id   : AP-ICET-{YEAR}
pattern_id: AP-ICET-PAT-V2

## Exam Pattern
exam_id: AP-ICET-{YEAR}
duration: 150 min | total_marks: 200 | neg: 0

| Section | Subject | Q | Marks |
|---|---|---|---|
| Section A — Analytical Ability | LR, DI | 75 | 75 |
| Section B — Mathematical Ability | MATH | 75 | 75 |
| Section C — Communication Ability | ENG | 50 | 50 |

### Section A Breakup
- Data Sufficiency: 20 Q
- Problem Solving: 55 Q

### Section B Breakup
- Arithmetical Ability: 35 Q
- Algebraical & Geometrical Ability: 30 Q
- Statistical Ability: 10 Q

### Section C Breakup
- Vocabulary: 10 Q | Business & Computer Terminology: 10 Q
- Functional Grammar: 15 Q | Reading Comprehension: 15 Q

## Subject Codes
LR, DI, MATH, ENG

## QID Types
S (mcq_single)

## Key Notes
- No negative marking
- Gateway for MBA and MCA admissions in all AP universities
- Graduation required (any stream for MBA; Math/Stats/CS for MCA)
- Conducted by APSCHE (AP State Council of Higher Education)
- Same pattern as TS ICET — separate rank list for AP counseling
- Valid only for AP university admissions in the same academic year
