## Key Notes
- No negative marking
- Qualifying marks: 60% (SC/ST/BC/PH: 50%)
- Certificate valid for 7 years (may be revised to lifetime)
- Mandatory for teaching in TS government and government-aided schools
- Conducted by Board of School Education, Telangana
- Separate from CTET — TSTET is Telangana-specific
