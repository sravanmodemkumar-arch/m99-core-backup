## Key Notes
- Recruitment to TS government school teachers
- TSTET qualifying mandatory before DSC
- No interview — merit based on written test + academic qualifications
- Academic merit component: 20% of DSC marks + 80% written
- D.Ed. required for SGT | B.Ed. required for SA/LP
- Conducted irregularly by state government based on vacancies
