## Key Notes
- Entry-level clerical posts in TS government departments
- No interview — merit based on written test
- Intermediate (Class 12) required; some posts require Class 10
- Age: 18–44 years (relaxation for reserved categories)
- Telugu language proficiency mandatory; Urdu medium option available
- Typing test conducted separately after written test selection
