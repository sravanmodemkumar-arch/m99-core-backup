## Key Notes
- Telangana State Public Service Commission — premier state exam
- Posts: Deputy Collector, DSP, Mandal Revenue Officer, etc.
- Strong emphasis on Telangana-specific GS (history, economy, governance)
- Graduation required; Age: 18–44 years (relaxation for reserved categories)
- Mains + Interview shortlisting: 1:3 ratio per post
