## Key Notes
- No negative marking
- Gateway for LLB admissions in all Telangana law colleges
- LAWCET: Graduation required | PGLCET: LLB required
- Conducted by TSCHE
- Rank-based counseling for seat allotment
- Aptitude for Law section tests logical/legal reasoning — no prior law knowledge needed for LAWCET
