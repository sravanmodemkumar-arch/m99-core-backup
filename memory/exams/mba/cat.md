# CAT (Common Admission Test)

board_id  : IIM
category  : C
exam_id   : CAT-{YEAR}
pattern_id: CAT-PAT-V5

## Exam Pattern
exam_id: CAT-{YEAR}
duration: 120 min (40 min per section) | total_marks: 198 | neg: -1 (MCQ only)

| Section | Subject | Q | MCQ | TITA | Marks |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Verbal Ability & Reading Comprehension | ENG | 24 | 19 | 5 | 72 |
| Data Interpretation & Logical Reasoning | DI, LR | 22 | 15 | 7 | 66 |
| Quantitative Ability | MATH | 22 | 14 | 8 | 66 |

Note: MCQ = +3/-1 | TITA (Type In The Answer) = +3/0

## Subject Codes
ENG, DI, LR, MATH

## QID Types
S (mcq_single) | T (tita — numeric answer)

## Key Notes
- Conducted by IIMs on rotational basis
- Gateway to IIMs and 1200+ MBA programs in India
- Sectional time limit: 40 min each (cannot switch between sections)
- Score scaled to percentile; 99+ percentile for top IIMs
- Graduation required (final year appearing allowed)
- No age limit
- Used by IITs, NITIE, SPJIMR, MDI, and most top B-schools
