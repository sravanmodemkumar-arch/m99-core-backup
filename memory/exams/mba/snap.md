# SNAP (Symbiosis National Aptitude Test)

board_id  : SIU
category  : C
exam_id   : SNAP-{YEAR}-{SESSION}
pattern_id: SNAP-PAT-V3

## Exam Pattern
exam_id: SNAP-{YEAR}
duration: 60 min | total_marks: 60 | neg: -0.25

| Section | Subject | Q | Marks |
|---|---|---|---|
| General English | ENG | 15 | 15 |
| Analytical & Logical Reasoning | LR, REAS | 25 | 25 |
| Quantitative-Data Interpretation & DS | MATH, DI | 20 | 20 |

## Subject Codes
ENG, LR, REAS, MATH, DI

## QID Types
S (mcq_single)

## Key Notes
- Conducted by Symbiosis International University (SIU)
- 3 attempts per year allowed; best score considered
- Accepts scores for SIBM Pune, SCMHRD, SIIB and 15+ Symbiosis institutes
- Graduation required, Age: no limit (some institutes have age limit)
- Sectional time limits: none (60 min total)
- Special section (15 marks, 0 negative) removed in recent years
