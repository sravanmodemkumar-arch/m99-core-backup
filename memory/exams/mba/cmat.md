## Exam Pattern
exam_id: CMAT-{YEAR}
duration: 180 min | total_marks: 400 | neg: -1

| Section | Subject | Q | Marks |
|---|---|---|---|
| Quantitative Techniques & Data Interpretation | MATH, DI | 20 | 80 |
| Logical Reasoning | LR | 20 | 80 |
| Language Comprehension | ENG | 20 | 80 |
| General Awareness | GK | 20 | 80 |
| Innovation & Entrepreneurship (optional) | INNOV | 20 | 80 |

Note: Innovation section optional; best 4 sections counted for merit in most institutes

## Subject Codes
MATH, DI, LR, ENG, GK, INNOV

## QID Types
S (mcq_single)

## Key Notes
- Conducted by NTA — accepted by AICTE-approved MBA institutes nationwide
- +4 marks per correct answer, -1 for incorrect
- Innovation & Entrepreneurship: optional 5th section (some institutes require it)
- Graduation required; Age: no limit
- Accepted by 1000+ AICTE-approved institutes
- Single session per year
