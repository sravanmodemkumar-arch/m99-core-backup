## Key Notes
- Conducted by XLRI Jamshedpur — used by XLRI and 160+ institutes
- Decision Making section is unique to XAT (not in CAT)
- Negative marking for unattempted after 8 questions (-0.05 per unattempted)
- No sectional time limit for Part 1 (175 min total)
- Graduation required; Age: no limit
- XLRI uses XAT score for BM (Business Management) and HRM programs
