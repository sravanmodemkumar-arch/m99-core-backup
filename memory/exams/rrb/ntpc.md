## Key Notes

- PG posts: Traffic Assistant, Senior Commercial Apprentice (Graduation)
- CBT 1: qualifying + shortlisting | CBT 2: merit
- Skill test / CBAT (for some posts) after CBT 2
- Typing test for clerical posts
