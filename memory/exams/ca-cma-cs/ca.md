# CA (Chartered Accountant)

board_id  : ICAI
category  : C

## CA Foundation
exam_id: CA-FOUNDATION-{YEAR}-{SESSION}
duration: 2 hrs per paper | neg: -0.25 (objective papers only)

| Paper | Subject | Marks | Type |
|---|---|---|---|
| Paper 1 | Principles & Practice of Accounting | 100 | Subjective |
| Paper 2 | Business Laws | 60 | Subjective |
| Paper 2 | Business Correspondence & Reporting | 40 | Subjective |
| Paper 3 | Business Mathematics, LR & Statistics | 100 | Objective (MCQ) |
| Paper 4 | Business Economics | 60 | Objective (MCQ) |
| Paper 4 | Business & Commercial Knowledge | 40 | Objective (MCQ) |

## CA Intermediate
exam_id: CA-INTER-{YEAR}-{SESSION}
duration: 3 hrs per paper | neg: 0

Group 1: Accounting(100) + Corporate Laws(100) + Cost & Management Accounting(100) + Taxation(100)
Group 2: Advanced Accounting(100) + Auditing & Assurance(100) + Enterprise IT & SM(50+50)

## CA Final
exam_id: CA-FINAL-{YEAR}-{SESSION}
duration: 3 hrs per paper | neg: 0

Group 1: FR(100) + SFM(100) + Advanced Auditing(100) + Corporate Laws(100)
Group 2: SCMPE(100) + Elective(100) + DT(100) + IDT(100)

## Subject Codes
ACCT, LAW, MATH, ECO, COST, TAX, AUDIT, FIN, IT

## QID Types
S (mcq_single) | D (descriptive/subjective)

## Key Notes
- Conducted by ICAI twice a year (May and November)
- Pass percentage: Foundation ~30%, Inter ~15–20%, Final ~10–15%
- Articleship (3 years) mandatory between Inter and Final
- Group-wise passing allowed (attempt both groups together or separately)
- Class 12 required for Foundation; Foundation pass for Intermediate
