## CS Executive
exam_id: CS-EXECUTIVE-{YEAR}-{SESSION}
duration: 3 hrs per paper | neg: 0

Module 1: Jurisprudence & Interpretation of Laws(100) + Company Law(100) + Setting Up Business(100) + Financial & Strategic Management(100)
Module 2: Corporate Restructuring(100) + Economic & Commercial Laws(100) + Tax Laws & Practice(100) + Industrial Labour Laws(100)

## CS Professional
exam_id: CS-PROFESSIONAL-{YEAR}-{SESSION}
duration: 3 hrs per paper | neg: 0

Module 1: Advanced Company Law(100) + Secretarial Audit(100) + Corporate Restructuring(100)
Module 2: Information Technology(100) + Financial & Treasury Management(100) + Ethics & Sustainability(100)
Module 3: Elective (100) + others

## Subject Codes
LAW, MGMT, ECO, ACCT, TAX, FIN, IT

## QID Types
S (mcq_single) | D (descriptive)

## Key Notes
- Conducted by ICSI (Institute of Company Secretaries of India)
- Twice a year: June and December
- 15 months training after Executive + 1 year practical training
- Company Secretary = key compliance officer in companies
- Class 12 for Foundation; Foundation pass for Executive
- Pass % varies: Foundation ~35%, Executive ~20–25%, Professional ~15–20%
