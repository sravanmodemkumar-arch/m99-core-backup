# CMA (Cost and Management Accountant)

board_id  : ICMAI
category  : C
exam_id   : CMA-{FOUNDATION|INTER|FINAL}-{YEAR}-{JUN|DEC}
pattern_id: CMA-FOUNDATION-PAT-V2 / CMA-INTER-PAT-V2 / CMA-FINAL-PAT-V2

## CMA Foundation
exam_id: CMA-FOUNDATION-{YEAR}-{SESSION}
duration: 2 hrs per paper | neg: -1 (for objective papers)

| Paper | Subject | Marks | Type |
|---|---|---|---|
| Paper 1 | Fundamentals of Economics & Management | 100 | Objective |
| Paper 2 | Fundamentals of Accounting | 100 | Objective |
| Paper 3 | Fundamentals of Laws & Ethics | 100 | Objective |
| Paper 4 | Fundamentals of Business Mathematics & Statistics | 100 | Objective |

## CMA Intermediate
exam_id: CMA-INTER-{YEAR}-{SESSION}
duration: 3 hrs per paper | neg: 0

Group 1 (4 papers, 100 each): Financial Accounting + Laws & Ethics + Direct Taxation + Cost Accounting
Group 2 (4 papers, 100 each): Operations Management + Financial Management + Indirect Taxation + Company Accounts

## CMA Final
exam_id: CMA-FINAL-{YEAR}-{SESSION}
duration: 3 hrs per paper | neg: 0

Group 3 (4 papers): Corporate Laws + SMA + DT + Indirect Tax Laws
Group 4 (4 papers): Corporate Financial Reporting + Financial Risk Management + Management Audit + Research Methodology/Elective

## Subject Codes
ECO, MGMT, ACCT, LAW, MATH, COST, TAX, FIN, AUDIT

## QID Types
S (mcq_single) | D (descriptive)

## Key Notes
- Conducted by ICMAI (Institute of Cost Accountants of India)
- Twice a year: June and December
- 15 months practical training (Practical Training Programme) required
- Pass % lower than CA but more focused on cost/management accounting
- Class 10 pass for Foundation; Foundation pass for Intermediate
