| Section | Subject | Q | Marks |
|---|---|---|---|
| Logical Reasoning | LR | 10–15 | 1 each |

Note: Question count varies by class (1–12). Achievers section = high-order thinking.

## Key Notes
- Conducted by SOF (Science Olympiad Foundation)
- Classes 1–12 eligible
- No negative marking
- Medals + certificates + cash prizes for top performers
- International ranking against students from 60+ countries
- Level 2 top performers represent India in international rounds
- Other SOF olympiads: NSO (Science), IEO (English), IGKO (GK)
