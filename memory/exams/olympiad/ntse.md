## Stage 1 (State Level)
exam_id: NTSE-S1-{YEAR}
duration: 120 min | total_marks: 200 | neg: 0

| Paper | Subject | Q | Marks |
|---|---|---|---|
| Paper 1 — MAT | Mental Ability Test | 100 | 100 |
| Paper 2 — SAT | Scholastic Aptitude Test (Sci+SSt+Math) | 100 | 100 |

Note: Language test (qualifying) varies by state

## Stage 2 (National Level)
exam_id: NTSE-S2-{YEAR}
duration: 120 min | total_marks: 200 | neg: -1/3

| Paper | Subject | Q | Marks |
|---|---|---|---|
| Paper 1 — MAT | Mental Ability Test | 100 | 100 |
| Paper 2 — SAT | Scholastic Aptitude Test | 100 | 100 |

## Subject Codes
MAT, SCI, SST, MATH

## QID Types
S (mcq_single)

## Key Notes
- Class 10 students only
- No negative marking in Stage 1; -1/3 in Stage 2
- 2000 scholarships awarded nationally
- Scholarship: ₹1250/month (Class 11-12), ₹2000/month (UG/PG), UGC norms for PhD
- Stage 1 conducted by individual states; Stage 2 by NCERT
- One of the most prestigious scholarships for school students in India
