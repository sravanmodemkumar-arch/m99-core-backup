board_id  : NTA
category  : C
exam_id   : UGC-NET-{YEAR}-{SESSION}
pattern_id: UGC-NET-PAT-V3

## Exam Pattern
exam_id: UGC-NET-{YEAR}
duration: 180 min | total_marks: 300 | neg: 0

| Paper | Subject | Q | Marks |
|---|---|---|---|
| Paper 1 (Teaching & Research Aptitude) | TEACH, REAS | 50 | 100 |
| Paper 2 (Subject-specific) | SUBJ | 100 | 200 |

Note: Paper 1 and Paper 2 conducted in single session (no break)

## Subject Codes
TEACH, REAS, SUBJ (varies by subject — 80+ subjects available)

## QID Types
S (mcq_single)

## Key Notes
- No negative marking
- Qualifies for: Assistant Professor (NET) + Junior Research Fellowship (JRF — age ≤ 30)
- 83+ subjects including Humanities, Social Sciences, Sciences, Commerce, Management, Law, etc.
- Conducted twice a year (June and December cycles)
- Master's degree required (pursuing final year also eligible)
- Qualifying: 40th percentile Paper1+Paper2 combined (SC/ST/PwD: 35th)
- JRF: Top 6% of qualified NET candidates
