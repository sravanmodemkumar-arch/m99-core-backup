## QID Types

## Key Notes
- Only for Delhi government schools
- TGT: Graduation + B.Ed required | PGT: Post-graduation + B.Ed required
- CTET/DTET qualifying mandatory
- Subjects: English, Hindi, Maths, Science, Social Science, Sanskrit, etc.
- Age: 18–32 years (relaxation for reserved categories)
