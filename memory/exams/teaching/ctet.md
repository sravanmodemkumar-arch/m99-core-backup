## Key Notes
- No negative marking
- Certificate valid: lifetime (earlier 7 years — changed)
- Conducted twice a year (online mode since 2021)
- Qualifying marks: 60% (SC/ST: 55%)
- Mandatory for teaching in Central Govt schools (KVS, NVS, etc.)
- Both Paper 1 and Paper 2 can be attempted in same session
