board_id  : NTA
category  : C (Competitive)
exam_id   : NTA-JEE-MAIN-{YEAR}-{SESSION}   e.g. NTA-JEE-MAIN-2025-JAN

## Sessions
- JAN session → NTA-JEE-MAIN-2025-JAN
- APR session → NTA-JEE-MAIN-2025-APR

## Pattern
duration: 180 min | total_marks: 300 | neg: -1

| Section | Subject | Q Total | Attempt | Marks | Neg | Types |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Physics | PHY | 30 (20 MCQ + 10 Num) | 25 | 4 | -1 (MCQ) / 0 (Num) | S, N |
| Chemistry | CHEM | 30 (20 MCQ + 10 Num) | 25 | 4 | -1 (MCQ) / 0 (Num) | S, N |
| Mathematics | MATH | 30 (20 MCQ + 10 Num) | 25 | 4 | -1 (MCQ) / 0 (Num) | S, N |

optional_q: 5 per section (attempt any 5 of 10 numericals)

## Subject Codes
PHY, CHEM, MATH

## QID Types
S (mcq_single), N (numerical)

## Key Notes
- 2 attempts per year (Jan + Apr); best score considered
- No calculator allowed
- Computer-based test (CBT)
- Qualifying for JEE Advanced (top 2.5L rank)
- Paper 2A: B.Arch (MATH+Drawing+Aptitude)
- Paper 2B: B.Planning (MATH+Aptitude+Planning)
