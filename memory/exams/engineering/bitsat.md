# BITSAT

board_id  : BITSPILANI
category  : C (Competitive)
exam_id   : BITSPILANI-BITSAT-{YEAR}
pattern_id: BITSAT-PAT-V2

## Pattern
duration: 180 min | total_marks: 450 | neg: -1 per wrong

| Section | Subject | Q | Marks each |
|---|---|---|---|
| Physics | PHY | 40 | 3 |
| Chemistry | CHEM | 40 | 3 |
| English Proficiency | ENG | 15 | 3 |
| Logical Reasoning | REAS | 10 | 3 |
| Mathematics / Biology | MATH or BIO | 45 | 3 |

Total: 150Q (PCM) or 150Q (PCB)

## Bonus Questions
- If all 150 answered with time remaining → 12 extra bonus questions
- Bonus Q correct: +3 | wrong: -1
- Cannot go back after bonus section

## Subject Codes
PHY, CHEM, ENG, REAS, MATH, BIO

## QID Types
S (mcq_single)

## Key Notes
- Online, conducted by BITS Pilani
- For Pilani, Goa, Hyderabad campuses
- PCM for Engineering; PCB for Pharma (BIO replaces MATH)
- Class 12 minimum 75% aggregate required
