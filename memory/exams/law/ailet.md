## PG Program (LLM)
exam_id: AILET-PG-{YEAR}
duration: 90 min | total_marks: 150 | neg: -0.25
150 questions: Legal Knowledge (50) + Analytical Abilities (50) + English (50)

## Subject Codes
ENG, GK, LAW, LR, MATH

## QID Types
S (mcq_single)

## Key Notes
- Exclusively for NLU Delhi (National Law University, Delhi)
- One of the toughest law entrance exams — only ~70 UG seats
- Separate from CLAT; NLU Delhi does not participate in CLAT consortium
- Slightly different pattern from CLAT — not passage-based
- Class 12 (45% marks) required for UG; LLB required for PG
- Age: no limit
