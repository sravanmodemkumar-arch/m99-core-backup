## UG Program (5-year LLB)
exam_id: CLAT-UG-{YEAR}
duration: 120 min | total_marks: 120 | neg: -0.25

| Section | Subject | Q | Marks |
|---|---|---|---|
| English Language | ENG | 22–26 | 22–26 |
| Current Affairs & GK | GK | 28–32 | 28–32 |
| Legal Reasoning | LAW | 28–32 | 28–32 |
| Logical Reasoning | LR | 22–26 | 22–26 |
| Quantitative Techniques | MATH | 10–14 | 10–14 |

Note: Passage-based questions — not standalone MCQs

## PG Program (LLM)
exam_id: CLAT-PG-{YEAR}
duration: 120 min | total_marks: 120 | neg: -0.25
120 questions on Constitutional Law, Jurisprudence, and other law subjects

## Subject Codes
ENG, GK, LAW, LR, MATH

## QID Types
S (mcq_single — comprehension/passage-based)

## Key Notes
- Consortium of National Law Universities (NLUs)
- Admissions to 22 NLUs across India (NLSIU Bangalore, NALSAR Hyderabad, etc.)
- All questions are passage-based (comprehension style)
- No prior legal knowledge required for UG
- Class 12 required for UG | LLB required for PG
- Age: no upper limit (UG: 45 years for SC/ST)
